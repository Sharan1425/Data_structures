-----------------------Binary Search-----------------------
* step 1 --> sort the list
* step 2 --> take middle num of the list
* step 3 --> if middle num is greater than given number, eliminate the right side of the element and go to left side of the list 
* step 4 --> if middle num is less than given number, eliminate the left side of the element go to rigth side of the list

Time Complexity in Binary Search
    - Log(n) Best case scenario for searching the number

Time Complexity in Linear Search
    - O(n) worst case scenario for searching the number.
